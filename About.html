Our Story

"Years ago, I would go to the health food store to find creams with healthy ingredients.
Everything had parabens in the preservative as well as alcohols and other toxic ingredients.
At the time I chose to blend essential oils with pure coconut oil to use as a moisturizer.
I created this line of product to give people a healthy alternative, ingredients that heal the skin, ingredients that have a gentle imprint on the planet and are in harmony with both."
All products are made with Organic Base Oils, Unrefined Oils, Non-GMO ingredients, and vegan ingredients!

There are no parabens or chemicals found in our products, and our Palm Oil is Rainforest Alliance Certified!
